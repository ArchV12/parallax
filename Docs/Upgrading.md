There are two separate paths to upgrading.

The first, is via Research.  Research what produces the ship upgrades
from all 6 ship slot categories, these will be blueprints.

All ship upgrade blueprints require knowledge to unlock. When the knowledge value is enough
to unlock the blueprint, the player will get a pop up notification saying a new upgrade is available.
It could be a bit descriptive, like "New Sub-Light Engine upgrade available: Fusion Drive" or whtaever.

The Research panel will need to be completely redesigned.  Right now its very basic.  This could be a larger panel
that takes up a good chunk of the screen.  I'd like 6 rows (one for each category) of 5 "Cards" each card represents an upgrade item.
The cards for equipment the player currently has will have a green border, or something to indicate we have it.  The card after that one will be revealed and show the unlock requirements, any cards beyond that simply have a ? on them as they are completely unknown.
There should probably be a right pointing arrow between each card to show that it's path.
Clicking on a card that you have all the materials for will upgrade to that piece of equipment; and apply the green border and reveal the next card's requirements.
