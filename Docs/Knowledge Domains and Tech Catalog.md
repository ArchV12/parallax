# The Parallax Initiative

# Knowledge Domains & Technology Catalog

## First Pass Brainstorm

**Version:** 0.1
**Status:** Concept Exploration

---

# Overview

The Parallax Initiative separates progression into two interconnected systems:

## Knowledge Domains

Knowledge represents humanity's understanding of the universe.

Knowledge is gained through scientific activities and discoveries.

Knowledge is never spent.

Knowledge enables humanity to develop new technologies.

---

## Technology Catalog

Technology represents what humanity can build.

Technologies are unlocked when sufficient knowledge exists across one or more Knowledge Domains.

Technologies are not owned by a single science category.

Many of humanity's greatest breakthroughs require multiple scientific disciplines working together.

---

# System Relationship

```
Scientific Activities

        ↓

Knowledge Domains

        ↓

Technology Requirements

        ↓

Humanity Develops Prototype

        ↓

Player Builds First Prototype

        ↓

Technology Becomes Available
```

# Design Rules

## Knowledge

* Never spent
* Represents permanent human advancement
* Grows through exploration

## Technologies

* Require multiple knowledge domains
* Are unlocked automatically
* Require a first prototype
* Become purchasable afterward

## Progression

The player does not unlock technology.

The player creates the discoveries that allow humanity to unlock technology.
